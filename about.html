About Us
Bio

I am a seasoned UX designer driven by a fervent commitment to leveraging technology to redefine and enhance the world through a foundational approach rooted in first principles thinking and a problem-centric design philosophy.

Born and raised in Jaipur. I excel in meticulously crafting impeccable digital experiences, my one equation I always get right. My serendipitous journey into the realm of UI/UX design began with a Google certification. Fueled by a thirst for knowledge, I honed my skills by avidly consuming online tutorials. My practical experience took shape through a rewarding internship, where I had the exhilarating opportunity to redesign the Payrupya app, marking a significant milestone in my career.

My journey to UX design

During Covid-19 when all the things were shutdown, school and collages were also closed. It’s become very hard for me to do study. “Study can be done at quiet place”. It’s me I love to work in quit place. So, in evening as my regular routine I have a walk with my neighbor and we have talk regarding “Which technology have the best scope in future?” and he tell me about UX DESIGN, that was the first time I heard about UX DESIGN. Then being me I started doing research on the same. I find it interesting and enroll in Google UX Design Certification course.

After successfully completing Google’s certification , I found that I have gained theoretical knowledge but somewhere lacks in practical. So, to nurture my new found interest I start watching Youtube tutorials. From there I fell I should pursue my career in UX. Seriouly I’m not lying, I know its too late to get to know. But the truth is truth.

Then I found an good internship where I have to redesign Payrupya app. I have to make retailer panel and admin panel. It was very interesting project. I really love it, taking my first internship experience which was quite nice. I learnt many things from there specially from my graphic designer and digital marketer more than my COO. It’s very funny that my head who is COO, I learnt less from him. And it’s time to end of my internship I completed my work successfully and yes, my overall experience was great.
